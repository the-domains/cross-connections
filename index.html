Cross Connections

Be imitators of God, as beloved children. And walk in love, as Christ loved us and gave himself up for us. ~Ephesians 5:1

A CRUCIAL PIECE OF ADVICE

I think that there's a reason why we tend to not see these cross connections; it's the reason why it took me eight years of my Christian life to start seeing them.It has to do with how we read the Bible.

There is a disconnect going on in many of our Christian lives. On one hand, we know the gospel-- the good news that because Jesus died and rose to forgive our sins, all who trust in him receive eternal life. We know the gospel is how we "get saved;" we repent of our sins and trust in Christ, and we are "in," so to speak.
